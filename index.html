About Me

I'm an indie games developer and artist based in Oregon.

I've been thinking, writing, playing and designing games since I was a small-ish, young-ish boy, and at some point I'll be able to prove it.

I started experimenting with computers and game-design while using GameMaker probably 15 years ago. I've since graduated my interests to music composition, graphic design, sound design, art & animation; really, the entire skillset that surrounds game-making. And now that I'm, let's say something of an adult, I aim to put those skills into something real.
